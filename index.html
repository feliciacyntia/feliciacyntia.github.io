About

I am a 4th-semester Data Science student at Telkom University, actively exploring how raw data can be transformed into valuable insights for both business and personal decision-making.

Currently, I’m learning Python and Go, while also building a solid foundation in SQL and Microsoft Excel to support my analytical work. I’ve worked on several small projects involving data exploration, basic machine learning, visualization, and deriving insights from real datasets.

For me, learning data analytics is not just about mastering tools — it’s about sharpening problem-solving skills and developing a logical way of thinking. I believe that every project, no matter how simple, is a meaningful step toward becoming a more adaptive, analytical, and impactful data analyst.

I may still be early in my journey, but I’m deeply motivated to keep growing, learning, and taking on real-world challenges in the field of data.
